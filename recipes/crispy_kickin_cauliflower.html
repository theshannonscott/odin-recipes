Crispy Kickin' Cauliflower

Description

Crispy Kickin' Cauliflower recipe is a tender, crunchy, easy, and delicious way to enjoy oven fried chicken without the actual meat. This is a great option for you if you donâ€™t eat gluten, because you can make it gluten-free. Serve it with our tender homemade vegan buttermilk waffles for the best chicken & waffles recipe ever! Breaded oven fried cauliflower is a great plant-based alternative to fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 Medium Cauliflower
  • 1 cup flour + 3 TBS flour
  • 1.5 cups panko breadcrumbs (more if necessary)
  • 1 cup non-dairy milk (plus more if necessary)
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano (or dried herb of your choice)
  • 2 teaspoons Lawrys seasoning salt (okay to do without if you don't have it)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • Oil for brushing [optional]
